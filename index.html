Learn How to Watercolor Paint!

Hello! Do you want to learn a new hobby? Watercolor painting is a relaxing and affordable way to creatively express yourself. My goal at Green Leaf Illustrations is to introduce people to the wonderful world of watercolor. Please sign up for our email list and join me for four weeks of FREE lessons. You will learn how to paint some basic plants that you can find right in your backyard! Share your progress with me and other students by tagging #greenLeafLearn.
