About Me

Hello, my name is Lucie of Green Leaf Illustrations. I have been a watercolor artist for over a decade and teacher for about half that time. I have taught a wide variety of age and skills groups across the years. Through this four week program I would like to get you outside and painting the lovely plants right in your backyard. Tag your artwork on social media with the hashtag #greenLeafLearn so I can see all your beautiful work!
